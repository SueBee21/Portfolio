About Me

I am a January 2021 graduate of University of Connecticut's Coding Certificate Bootcamp.

After over 15 years working in healthcare and community non-profit organizations as a clinical social worker, I am excited to take my problem-solving skills into a new line of work. Although I only recently entered the world of coding, I have found myself quickly immersed and eager to learn more about the creative potential of programming languages.

I am an avid and adventurous learner, recognizing that there is great value in choosing to actively pursue challenges in life. In recent years I have dedicated my focus to pursue challenges in physical endurance - having completed marathons, triathlons, mountain and trail races in the past 6 years. I am also a dabbling musician, having learned violin, guitar, and clarinet in younger years, and recently choosing to learn the viola. It was in this pursuit, where I was challenged to learn to read music in a new clef, that I discovered the value of expanding my mind in new directions. In this same vein, I am excited to take on this new challenge to learn programming languages and begin a new career in the expanding world of tech.
